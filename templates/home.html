Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

What format do my traces need to be in?

We accept any JSON format that represents a conversation history — the standard messages array [{role, content}] works perfectly. If you include tool calls, we'll evaluate them too. Email us if your agent uses a proprietary format.

Do you support all AI frameworks?

Yes. Framework-agnostic by design. LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen, raw Anthropic or OpenAI API calls — it doesn't matter. No vendor dependencies.

What's the difference between this and Braintrust or LangSmith?

Those tools are excellent for automated monitoring and developer iteration. We solve a different problem: delivering a structured third-party assessment with human expert review that's credible to non-technical stakeholders. Many of our clients use both.

What do human reviewers actually do?

They read the full conversation trace, see the LLM judge's verdict and reasoning, and submit an independent PASS or FAIL with written notes. Clinical traces are reviewed by professionals with clinical backgrounds. Their verdict is the ground truth.

How is the pass rate calculated?

Overall pass rate = PASS traces / total traces. We also report by task type, conversation length, and tool usage pattern. Primary metric is pass@1 — did the agent succeed on the first attempt?

What happens after the audit?

PDF report + 45-minute debrief call. We walk through findings and help translate recommendations into your team's backlog. Most clients book a follow-up audit 4–8 weeks later.

Is my data kept confidential?

Yes. Reviewers sign NDAs. We don't train models on your data. We don't share findings with third parties. We can sign a mutual NDA before you share any traces.

Can we do a smaller trial first?

Yes. A 5-trace sample evaluation for $500. You'll see our scoring methodology, report format, and reviewer quality before committing. Email hello@agenteval.com.
